Song of Moses

Deuteronomy 32:3-6, 10-14, 18 October 9, 2022

KJV

  1. 3Because I will publish the name of the Lord:
  2. Ascribe ye greatness unto our God.
  3. 4He is the Rock, his work is perfect:
  4. For all his ways are judgment:
  5. A God of truth and without iniquity,
  6. Just and right is he.
  7. 5They have corrupted themselves, their spot is not the spot of his children:
  8. They are a perverse and crooked generation.
  9. 6Do ye thus requite the Lord,
  10. O foolish people and unwise?
  11. Is not he thy father that hath bought thee?
  12. Hath he not made thee, and established thee?
  1. 10He found him in a desert land,
  2. And in the waste howling wilderness;
  3. He led him about, he instructed him,
  4. He kept him as the apple of his eye.
  5. 11As an eagle stirreth up her nest,
  6. Fluttereth over her young,
  7. Spreadeth abroad her wings, taketh them,
  8. Beareth them on her wings:
  9. 12So the Lord alone did lead him,
  10. And there was no strange god with him.
  11. 13He made him ride on the high places of the earth,
  12. That he might eat the increase of the fields;
  13. And he made him to suck honey out of the rock,
  14. And oil out of the flinty rock;
  15. 14Butter of kine, and milk of sheep,
  16. With fat of lambs, and rams of the breed of Bashan,
  17. And goats, with the fat of kidneys of wheat;
  18. And thou didst drink the pure blood of the grape.
  1. 18Of the Rock that begat thee thou art unmindful,
  2. And hast forgotten God that formed thee.

NIV

  1. 3I will proclaim the name of the Lord.
  2. Oh, praise the greatness of our God!
  3. 4He is the Rock, his works are perfect,
  4. and all his ways are just.
  5. A faithful God who does no wrong,
  6. upright and just is he.
  1. 5They are corrupt and not his children;
  2. to their shame they are a warped and crooked generation.
  3. 6Is this the way you repay the Lord,
  4. you foolish and unwise people?
  5. Is he not your Father, your Creator,
  6. who made you and formed you?
  1. 10In a desert land he found him,
  2. in a barren and howling waste.
  3. He shielded him and cared for him;
  4. he guarded him as the apple of his eye,
  5. 11like an eagle that stirs up its nest
  6. and hovers over its young,
  7. that spreads its wings to catch them
  8. and carries them aloft.
  9. 12The Lord alone led him;
  10. no foreign god was with him.
  1. 13He made him ride on the heights of the land
  2. and fed him with the fruit of the fields.
  3. He nourished him with honey from the rock,
  4. and with oil from the flinty crag,
  5. 14with curds and milk from herd and flock
  6. and with fattened lambs and goats,
  7. with choice rams of Bashan
  8. and the finest kernels of wheat.
  9. You drank the foaming blood of the grape.
  1. 18You deserted the Rock, who fathered you;
  2. you forgot the God who gave you birth.
